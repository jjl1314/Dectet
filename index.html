Our Mission

Devils Dectet is a student-led ensemble committed to sharing the beauty and power of ensemble music with schools and local audiences. We believe music is a universal language that connects people, inspires creativity, and enriches communities.

I started Devils Dectet because I am a firm believer that music has powers within itself that are worth a lot more than money. Some of my favorite school memories have involved playing music with my friends, and I began to see the vision of a prelude group during sophomore year. The thing that separates our group from others is our dedication and trust in each other, as we are all in the same grade and have been friends for many years. We make sacrifices to ensure that we sound the best we can for each other, and our bond is what pushes us to make music together. I'm so happy I started this group, and I can't wait to keep performing with them!
